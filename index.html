What is Starbucks for Life?

Everything you need to know about this exciting annual holiday promotion

The Ultimate Coffee Sweepstakes

Starbucks for Life is an annual holiday game promotion run by Starbucks, typically starting around Thanksgiving and running through early January. It's one of the most anticipated promotions among coffee lovers!

During the game period, Starbucks Rewards members can collect game pieces for chances to win amazing prizes, including the grand prize of free Starbucks for 30 years.

How to Play Starbucks for Life

Follow these simple steps to start playing and winning

1

Join Starbucks Rewards

Download the Starbucks app or visit starbucks.com to create a free Starbucks Rewards account. This is required to participate in the game.

2

Earn Game Plays

Get plays by making purchases with your Rewards account, or claim free daily plays just by logging in. You can also enter via mail (AMOE) at no cost.

3

Collect Game Pieces

Use your plays to reveal game pieces. Collect all pieces in a set to win the corresponding prize. Each prize has common and rare pieces.

4

Win Prizes

Complete a set to win! Instant win prizes are automatically added to your account. Grand prize winners are contacted directly by Starbucks.

Winning Tips & Strategies

Maximize your chances with these proven strategies

📅

Play Every Day

Don't miss your free daily plays! Logging in consistently is the easiest way to collect more game pieces without spending extra money.

🌟

Watch for Bonus Play Days

Starbucks often offers double or bonus play promotions during the game period. Keep an eye on the app for special offers.

📈

Understand the Rare Pieces

Each prize set has one rare piece that's harder to find. Focus on collecting common pieces first - the rare piece is the key to winning.

💰

Don't Overspend

Play with your normal purchases. The odds are the same whether you buy one drink or ten. Enjoy the game without breaking the bank.

✉

Use Mail-In Entry

The Alternate Method of Entry (AMOE) lets you request free plays by mail. This is 100% free and gives you extra chances to win.

🔒

Keep Realistic Expectations

Grand prize odds are around 1 in 20 million. Enjoy the instant wins (bonus stars, free drinks) and treat the grand prize as a fun bonus possibility.

Starbucks for Life typically runs from mid-November through early January. The exact dates are announced on the Starbucks app and website. Sign up for Starbucks Rewards to get notified when the game begins.

Starbucks for Life is primarily available to residents of the United States and Canada. Participants must be legal residents and meet age requirements (typically 18+ or 19+ in some Canadian provinces).
